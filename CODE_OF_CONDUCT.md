# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We want **Anesk (Arcade Pulse — Snake)** and all **Web Weavers World** projects to be a safe, welcoming place for everyone — whether you’re brand new to coding or an experienced developer.  

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for all, regardless of age, gender identity, expression, sexual orientation, disability, neurodiversity, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, religion, or level of experience.

---

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior we encourage:
- Being kind and respectful in all interactions
- Offering constructive feedback, not personal attacks
- Welcoming new contributors and being patient with beginners
- Giving credit and recognition where it’s due

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal insults, trolling, or “flame wars”
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome

---

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Setting clear standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate, fair action when rules are broken
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this Code

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within the project repository (issues, pull requests, comments)
- In related spaces such as community discussions, chat, or other official project channels

---

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository maintainer ([@LilPwinc3ss554](https://github.com/LilPwinc3ss554)).  

All complaints will be reviewed and dealt with fairly. Maintainers are obligated to keep reports private.
